ABOUT

Who we are

Michael Miller, founder of MMS, has served in Long Term Care and Assisted Living communities for over a decade. As CEO of a well-known senior living community, he faced the need of a good medical staffing partner. Noticing deficiencies in the quality of service that agencies provided, along with low compensation to their staff, he was inspired to make a change in the perception that agency staff is just another "warm body" in the building. Miller Medical Staffing is raising the bar. We provide nothing less than exemplary services to our communities, and our team receives great pay and exciting new opportunities!

TEAM

What we do

Miller Medical Staffing exists to connect healthcare professionals with facilities in need of extra support. We provide services for Long-Term Care & Assisted Living communities.
